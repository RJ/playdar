Playdar is still new and under heavy development. It's 'Alpha', but is usable! There's plenty to get excited about - please join the mailing list if you have any questions or comments.

Playdar is a music content resolver service - run it on every computer you use, and you'll be able to listen to all the songs you would otherwise be able to find manually by searching though all your computers, hard disks, online services, and friends' music collections. Learn more...
